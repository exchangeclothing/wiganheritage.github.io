3. Letter from Wigan Council.

Pam Stewart asked Emma Scott to read out the letter received from Wigan Council regarding NRF funding.
Emma confirmed that she received a letter from Christine
Bainbridge (Resource Procurement & Development Manager, Wigan MBC), that confirms the end of the Neighbourhood Renewal funding for the Community Empowerment Programme at the end of March 2008.

The discussion raised the following points:

· CEP documentation will remain at CVS for 7 years.

· There needs to be an agreement for data protection issues with CVS to enable ENCOMPASS to continue to have database contacts available.

· Nick Trotter - CVS needs to clarify legal obligations about transfer of information to networks and "change of use" issues.

· Agreed to form a sub-group to discuss this and make proposals to take this forward. Emma, Nick, Pam, Carol and Irene agreed to do this. Also ENCOMPASS needs an "acting Secretary" due to Joe Taylor's absence for a few months. Volunteer wanted as soon as possible !

· Nick - Changes in the Structures of the Local Strategic Partnership and the Local Area Agreement are still not decided. This has implications for all the community empowerment networks and Encompass for representation on the thematic partnerships and the Board.

· Pam - this also raises serious questions about the Council's Beacon Council status

· Still queries about the roles and responsibilities of the Townships and PACTs

· Marion - On the structure diagram for the LSP, there is no direct link for community representation - it is a separate block. This is serious for the community networks and Encompass and needs to be addressed.

· Pam - There is a serious issue about the lack of recognition by Wigan MBC of the roles and all the work of volunteers throughout the CEP's work over the last 5 years.

· Nick - Volunteers should not be "out of pocket" for their time commitments and valuable participation in the CEP, LSP etc. Is the Council going to resource volunteers for their future participation ?

· Emma - Funding from the Esmee Fairbairn Foundation is a possibility - Marion, Clare, Carol and Pam agreed to meet to pursue this

· Carol - Networks need to investigate applying for funding independently as well as Encompass.

· Andrew suggested that all the networks and Encompass become members of CVS as a way of still being able access support and information beyond March 31st 2008.

· There is to be a meeting with Simon Dale to clarify many of the issues. Date to be arranged.
