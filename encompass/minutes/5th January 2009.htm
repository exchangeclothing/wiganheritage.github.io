Bank Account & Related Financial Matters:

A complete Excel record of all financial transactions, since the Encompass bank account was opened 23 Oct 2007, was presented and scrutinised in detail. Receipts have been obtained for all expenditure except the cheque paid Nov 2007 to Wigan District Community Transport for transport to the Community North West conference in Kendal. Those members wishing to claim expenses should forward their detailed expense claims for ratification, at their earliest convenience. Anne Roberts has been off sick and this had caused some delay. Anne was also helping to draft an agreement between Encompass and those recipients awarded funding at the Participatory Budgeting event with regards to conditions of use and accountability. Carol will make inquires tomorrow (6th Jan) re progress with this document and report back.

As the secretary will not be available for two weeks, the bankbook will be deposited in the safe at The Grange so that Khaled can collect it as necessary. Irene Yates is also a signatory to the account.

The secretary intends taking accounting training with St Helens CVS and it was decided that he should continue his role as acting treasurer until such time as another member gains a relevant accreditation and offers to take over the role.

Office Space - yes no or maybe:

The Next Ascend Meeting at the Town Hall:

Pam Stewart spoke about the rapid and productive progress made since the end of the CEP program and the break with CVS. Encompass is now a recognised entity in the Borough.

A concern is that we are losing long-established, respected community identities such as Eileen Waldron and having difficulty finding replacements. It is therefore important that we make a determined effort to bring new people into the local governance process and into Encompass.

Due in part to government directives regarding community involvement, the community networking consultancy, the developing community networking strategy and other changed circumstances, including funding, we are now at the beginning of a new era regarding the relationship between the community, the LSP and the delivery of services within the Borough. The next Ascend meeting will be a very important step in the building of that new relationship. After much constructive discussion it was decided that the secretary is delegated to arrange the next Ascend meeting in conjunction with the LSP chief officer and to keep the Chair and other members informed at all stages.

The Website:

A website is currently being developed for Encompass. Progress to that date was displayed at the last Community Networking Strategy meeting. Several people have since made contributions and suggested modifications have been made. Sian Jay has indicated that she approves of the site and inquired as to a marketing strategy for it. Clare Brennan and Khaled Amini have webpage design capacity and both approve of the site. Joe will teach Khaled how to update the site, when convenient. Joe affirmed the need to bring more local residents into the local governance process, as outlined under the 'Get In Touch' tab of the site http://www.enwal.org/index.php?page=get-in-touch (inspired by the Vision 2026 Community Strategy). Clare emphasised the need for a good marketing strategy for the site and the work that Encompass is now doing. Joe indicated that he thought to incorporate the launch of the site into the marketing strategy that will be developed for the next Ascend meeting.
