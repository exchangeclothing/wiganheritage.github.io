WELCOME TO THE THE WIGAN BOROUGH ENVIRONMENT & HERITAGE NETWORK

The Wigan Borough Environment & Heritage Network is the forum for anyone concerned with Environment, Heritage, History and Archaeology matters, throughout the entire Wigan Borough, to meet on a regular basis and  exchange news, views and aspirations

E & H Network meetings allow members of the public to be engaged in consultation with Leisure & Culture Trust and other Council Departments, on matters concerning our Heritage.

If you are interested in attending a meeting or becoming a member of this group, please contact the secretary by completing this form:

E & H Network is one component of the Community Network, of which Encompass is the strategic body

It forms a part of the overall structure described in the Local Area Agreement (LAA)
